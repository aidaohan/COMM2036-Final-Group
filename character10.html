Erlang Shen

Erlang Shen can only be reached after doing Erlang, the Sacred Divinity's questline. Erlang then can be found in Mount Mei, waiting at its peak. Once defeated, he will turn into Erlang Shen and will let The Four Heavenly Kings fight the Destined One. Once they are defeated as well, he himself will face the Destined One.
