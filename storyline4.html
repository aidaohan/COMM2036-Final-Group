Chapter Four
Rosy Cheeks,Gray Hair

The Second Sister captures Zhu Bajie for the Violet Spider. The Destined One rescues him. After the Violet Spider is defeated, she runs to the Hundred-Eyed Daoist Master, who will betray her and take back the "Envious Tongue." The spider sisters and the Destined One defeat the Hundred-Eyed Daoist Master. Zhu Bajie glances at the injured Violet Spider and sadly walks to the relic. The Chosen One and Zhu Bajie make their way to the Flaming Mountains.

Our Thoughts:
In chapter 4, we will find the action aggregate, which is divided into body action, speech action, and mind action. The actions of people who are blinded by ignorance will naturally end badly.
